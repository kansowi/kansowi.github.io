About me

Design consciousness is the bridge between all the disciplines and my passion is to design products that are purposeful, ergonomic and aesthetically pleasing.

With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, Neharika's key skills include prototyping, research and analysis, conceptualization, and marketing. Her adaptive nature, effective communication, calm demeanor, keen observation, and strategic planning set her apart.
